VISION

To create an environment where every individual, regardless of their economic or social situation, has access to quality health care for the prevention and treatment of cancer, thereby enabling a significant improvement in public health and quality of life.

MISSION

We work to raise awareness, improve access to care, train healthcare professionals, support patients, promote research, and strengthen cancer-fighting infrastructure in the DRC.

OUR CORE OBJECTIVE

Reducing cancer mortality in the Democratic Republic of Congo is our main objective

Our Values

EMPATHY

We are committed to listening and understanding the unique challenges young people with cancer face, offering sincere and compassionate support.

SOLIDARITY

Together, we join forces to support young people and their families through every stage of their journey, building a strong and united network of care.

RESPECT

we value each individual and respect their unique journey, creating an inclusive and caring environment.

DIGNITY

We strive to ensure each young person is treated with honor and compassion, preserving their self-worth through difficult times.
