WE ARE

We are young people dedicated to the fight against cancer.

We envision an environment where every individual, regardless of their economic or social status, has access to quality health care for the prevention and treatment of cancer.

Learn more...

AREAS OF FOCUS

We work across diverse areas to create lasting impact. Explore our fields of action below.

Prevention and Awareness

We implement awareness campaigns nationwide, especially in rural and vulnerable areas.

Research and Innovation

We fund and publish local research on prevalent cancers and accessible treatments in the DRC.

Support for Patients

We offer psychological and material help to patients and families, working with professionals.

Equitable Access to Care

We advocate for accessible early diagnosis and treatment for all citizens.
