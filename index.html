WE ARE

We are young people dedicated to the fight against cancer

We envision an environment where every individual, regardless of their economic or social status, has access to quality health care for the prevention and treatment of cancer, thereby enabling a significant improvement in public health and quality of life.

Learn more...

AREAS OF FOCUS

We work across diverse areas to create lasting impact. Explore our fields of action below.

Prevention and awareness

We are implementing awareness campaigns nationwide, with a particular focus on rural areas and the most vulnerable communities.

Research and innovation

Fight Cancer encourages and funds local research on the most common types of cancer in the DRC, as well as on treatment approaches adapted to local resources. The results of this research are published and shared with the national and international medical community.

Support for patients and their families

We are committed to providing psychological, material and moral support to cancer patients and their families, in partnership with psychologists and social workers.

Equitable access to care

We advocate for all citizens, regardless of income level or geographic location, to have access to early diagnosis and adequate treatment.
